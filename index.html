Discover Ethiopia

History: Ethiopia, one of the oldest nations in the world, has a rich history dating back thousands of years. Known for being the birthplace of coffee and the ancient kingdom of Aksum, Ethiopia boasts a unique cultural heritage and is home to the rock-hewn churches of Lalibela and other UNESCO World Heritage sites.

Facts: Ethiopia is the only African country never colonized, and it has its own calendar, which is about 7-8 years behind the Gregorian calendar. It’s also known for its diverse landscapes, including the Great Rift Valley, highlands, and volcanic lakes, which contribute to its incredible biodiversity.
